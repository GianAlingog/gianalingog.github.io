My Interests

I was only four years old when my father taught me how to play chess. My growth remained slow and my strength was stagnant until I finally got into my school varsity team at ten years old. Although I was steadily getting better, I hit a skill ceiling. It all changed when I became fourteen. I kept pushing the limits, constantly playing more games, and reading more books. I finally broke the 2000 elo barrier online on lichess last November 2, 2022. Yet, my journey doesn't end here. I'm currently working on my FIDE rating and title, and I plan to be a much more active chess player once I move to the USCF.
